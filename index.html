MetaBreaker.gg

Looking for unique and off-meta builds for your favorite champions in League of Legends? Look no further than MetaBreaker.gg - the item build generator created by myself and four talented friends.

Our web application is the perfect tool for those looking to spice up their gameplay with unconventional builds. Utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, MetaBreaker is both sleek and user-friendly. Plus, our integration with Riot Games Data Dragon ensures that all data on champions and items is accurate and up-to-date.
